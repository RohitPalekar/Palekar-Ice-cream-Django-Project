Our Story

Good taste is important. Honest ingredients, even more so. Which is why, at Palekar,s Ice Creams, we make it our mission to bring you the most delightful of ice creams, ranging from the exotic to the innovative.

We have crafted flavours ranging from fruit and dry fruit variations like Tender Coconut, Alphonso Mango, Dry Fruit Overload, Roasted Almonds to international flavors such as Mediterranean Sea Salted Caramel, Madagascar Chocolate, French Vanilla, and those inspired by Indian sweetmeats such as Gulab Jamun, Sheer Khurma, Til Gud, and Gajar Halwa, to name a few.

In our entire range of offerings, we use only milk and 100% ingredients. No artificial flavors, no coloring agents and zero preservatives: 'Honestly' is not just our motto, it’s also our promise. Through the use of milk and our hand-crafted production process, we bring you a range of enthralling tastes that you shall find only with Palekar,s Ice Creams.

Our Process

All ingredients are hand-picked, the fruits are manually cleaned and whole fruits are used in production. That’s the reason why we call it 'Handcrafted Ice Creams’. Since there are no preservatives in our ice cream, the shelf life of our product is just 45 days. When compared with other brands, which have a longer shelf life, it is a herculean effort to manufacture, distribute and sell our ice cream across the nation within 45 days. With more experience and better processes, we have been successful in overcoming various challenges to bring Palekar Icecream to you, making every challenge worth it and after tasting Palekar Icecream you surely would agree!
